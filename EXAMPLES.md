## How to Use These Examples

1. Copy any problem above
2. Run `python main.py`
3. Paste the problem when prompted
4. Get step-by-step solution
5. Ask follow-up questions for better understanding

---

## Tips for Complex Problems

- **Be specific:** "JEE Advanced level quadratic" instead of just "quadratic"
- **Give context:** "This is from the chapter on limits"
- **Ask for methods:** "Show me both factorization and quadratic formula"
- **Request proofs:** "Can you prove why this works?"
